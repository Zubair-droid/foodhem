Mission

The mission of Foodhem is fighting the best against hunger and food wastage. The World Health Organisation (WHO) cites malnutrition as the gravest single threat to the world’s public health and the biggest contributor to child mortality in the world.

The world keeps on fighting the hunger and Foodhem is taking part in the fight against hunger is a privilege to us

About

Foodhem is a non-profit online platform which connects the people going under shortages of food which is not even able to suffice their needy and the people who are often come around with the stoppage of large amount of food after their consumption and the satisfaction. So, connecting them in-person and knowing, foodhem helps in donating food to the required and on the otherhand the people can request food here, so can the suitable donor gets connected thereby the surplus food can be resdistributed. The main objective of Foodhem is getting the food scarcity and wastages lines gradually down, so people get benefitted.

How Foodhem work

The users of foodhem can categorize themselves as donors and recepients as they sign up.

The donors are asked to add the data of surplus food which they are looking to donate

The recepients can add request by asking their needs so that donor can find their suitable recepients

This way both the sides get into contact and ship accordingly.
